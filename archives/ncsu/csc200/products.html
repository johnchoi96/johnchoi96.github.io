Products

Our company offers three different products:

  • Ground coverage only: this package will only provide artificial intelligence for the ground control of the airport. This AI will control aircrafts that are on the ground from the runway to the gate or vice versa. This package will be $2.3 million.




  • Airspace coverage only: this package will only provide artificial intelligence for the airspace control near the airport. This AI will direct aircrafts that are in the air to their destination or to the runway for them to land. The AI will know when to redirect aircrafts based on the traffic on the runway or the weather condition. This package will cost $3.1 million.





  • Full coverage: this package will provide artificial intelligence to cover both ground and airspace of the airport. This will potentially eliminate the need for human intervention to run the airport and at the same time, reduce the chance of air disaster caused by a human error. This package will cost $4.7 million.
