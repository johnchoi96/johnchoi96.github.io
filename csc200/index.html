About Aviation Interaction:

    Aviation Interaction, founded by John Choi initially as a part of NC State University in 2016, is one of the leading modern aviation software company. It puts a substantial amount of effort to improve the current air traffic system. Its products continue to make the airspace safer and worry-free. Recently, it was recognized as a leading aerospace engineering company with its new product, ATC A.I. The company is expected to bring the air traffic death rates down to 1% by 2020.


Goals and Missions:

    Our goal here at Aviation Interaction is to implement artificial intelligences into the Air Traffic Control system. Doing this reduces the chances that humans can make, preventing a potential disaster on the airport's runway. In fact, the statistics show that about 58% of the aircraft accidents are caused by a human error. Implementing an artificial intelligence into the ATC would greatly reduce this percentage. Thus, our goal is to engineer an artificial intelligence that is specifically designed to operate an air traffic control with its perfect algorithms and logic.
